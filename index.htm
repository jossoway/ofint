Welcome to Oilfield International

Established in 1990, Oilfield International offers technical, valuation, acquisition and contracts services to oil companies, and to companies entering the oil industry by acquisition or startup.

We have hands-on, on-site, experience of working in Russia/CIS; the North Sea; and the Middle East, especially Iraq.

Our consultants all have more than 20 years' world-wide experience, senior professional qualifications and between one and three university degrees.
