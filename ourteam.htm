Geophysicist and BD South America

David Curia (MS Applied Mathematics, MS Geophysics) has a solid geophysical technical experience and a proven career track record. Steven Foster

Geophysicist

Steve Foster [USA, BS Geology (UC Davis), MS Earth Science (M.I.T.)] is a petroleum geophysicist. He spent 25 years with Unocal, 14 of those at overseas postings in Norway, Argentina, Syria, and Thailand doing exploration and development geophysics as a seismic interpreter and District Geophysicist; while with Unocal in Los Angeles he also worked the North Sea, Egypt, Sudan, Japan, and most of Latin America. Since retiring from Unocal he has had foreign assignments in Mexico and Argentina, and worked onshore Nevada and offshore Namibia.
